Biology Quiz

1. What is the Doppler effect?

  • a) The change in pitch of a sound wave
  • b) The change in frequency of a wave due to relative motion
  • c) The reflection of waves off a surface
  • d) The diffraction of waves around obstacles

Correct Answer: b) The change in frequency of a wave due to relative motion
